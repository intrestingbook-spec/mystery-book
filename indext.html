You Are Invited

Five strangers are summoned to a gothic estate.
A billionaire is declared dead.
The will is a threat. The inheritance is a trap.
Only one of them knows the truth.

The Heirs

Trevor Vance

A ruthless business mogul obsessed with control and legacy.

Anya Bellweather

A professor driven by logic, hiding emotional fractures.

Chloe Finch

An ordinary barista pulled into extraordinary darkness.

Marcus Cross

A journalist chasing truth, haunted by abandonment.

Sara Bellweather

A quiet observer who understands chaos better than anyone.

The Summons

Day 1, The drawing room. The five heirs have just heard the bizarre terms of the will and the threatening first recording from the "deceased," Silas Bellweather. The atmosphere is hostile and terrified.


Trevor

(Pacing aggressively, running a hand through his expensive hair)


This is insane. An explicit threat? A scavenger hunt? I have a multi-billion-dollar acquisition closing Friday. I will not be trapped in a gothic horror show with strangers.

Anya

(Calmly folding her hands)


The threat is serious, Mr. Vance. Bellweather clearly suggests one of us had motive enough to kill him. We should be focused on finding the connection. Where did you know Silas Bellweather from?

Trevor

(Scoffs)


I didn't. I received a certified letter from some shell corporation naming me an heir. I assumed it was a standard legal fight. Clearly, I assumed wrong.

Chloe

(Whispering, intimidated by the grandeur of the room)


I work at a coffee shop. In Portland. I spilled coffee on the wrong lawyer, maybe? That's my only connection to high finance.

Marcus

(Scribbling furiously in a small notebook)


Vellum said Bellweather died two months ago. If one of us killed him, the body is already cold. What we need to understand is why he left this maze. It's not about the money, it's about making a spectacle. And why five strangers?

Sara

(Staring out the window at the storm, her voice low and even)


He needs us here. All five of us. If it were just about money, he would have left it to a foundation. He set this up so we would tear the place, and each other, apart. He built his fortune on chaos; he's distributing it the same way.

Trevor

That's ridiculous, Sara. He built it on shrewd, cold business. You sound like a conspiracy theorist.

Marcus

(Looking up sharply at Sara)


Maybe she's right. If we're all pawns, we need to figure out the board. Before one of us becomes the next victim in his little game.

The Revelation of the Siblings

Day 3. Trevor has just collapsed (the "poisoning"). While searching his belongings for a clue, the remaining four discover identical birth certificates and letters linking them to Silas Bellweather.


Anya

[Anya holds up five separate, sealed envelopes found hidden beneath the lining of Trevor's expensive briefcase. They each contain old birth certificates.]


(Voice shaking, reading the documents)


Marcus, yours says... mother: Diane Cross. Father: Anonymous. Born: New York, 1985. Chloe, yours... mother: Laura Finch. Father: Anonymous. Born: Los Angeles, 1986. Mine...


[She trails off, placing her own document down. Marcus snatches his.]

Marcus

(Reading rapidly, his face draining of color)


The dates. They're all within three years of each other. Five different mothers. Five different cities. All "Anonymous" fathers. But look at the signature on the back of Chloe's form-a notary stamp from Bellweather's old corporation.

Chloe

(Tears welling up)


He didn't know us. He didn't want us. He just... collected us.

Anya

(Her scientific detachment crumbling)


This wasn't a competition for money. This was a convergence. He brought his estranged children together for one final, vicious act. We aren't strangers, Marcus. We are siblings.

Sara

(A cold whisper, not looking at anyone)


Siblings who hate each other. Siblings who, thanks to him, now suspect one of us tried to kill the other. It's perfect. It strips away every ounce of humanity and forces us to be what he always was: ruthless.

Marcus

(Clutching his own birth certificate, the journalist faÃ§ade gone)


He wasn't testing us for an inheritance. He was testing us to see who was enough like him to survive this kind of cruelty. He created a murder mystery out of our abandonment.

Chloe

(Looking around the richly decorated, yet cold, room)


But who killed him? If we're his family, why would we murder him before the will? We had no idea he was our father until now!

Anya

(Looking pointedly at Sara)


Unless one of us did know. Unless one of us found the truth years ago, and came here not for the money, but for the end.

The Confrontation

Day 4, The Underground Archive. Silas Bellweather has just revealed he is alive and that he set up the game. Sara has been exposed as the true killer of the real executor, Mr. Vellum.


[Silas's holographic image flickered, his smug expression replaced by a flash of disbelief as Anya and Chloe rejected his game. Sara stood rigid by the control panel, ready to claim the fortune.]


Silas

(A sharp, disbelieving laugh echoing through the room)


You-you pathetic fools! You actually choose justice over power? Anya, you could fund your own university! Marcus, you could buy the network that fired you! You reject the only path to true authority!

Marcus

(Stepping forward, his voice steady with conviction)


Your authority is a lie, Silas. You faked your death to orchestrate a psychological torture session. You are nothing but a coward. And we are done playing your game.

Sara

(Eyes blazing at Marcus)


Don't you understand? If he walks away from this , he wins! The only way to silence him forever is to take his entire empire and collapse it! I killed the executor to keep this door open!

Chloe

(Moving swiftly, hacking into a system port)


And we're closing it. You wanted a public spectacle, Silas? Fine.


[Chloe frantically taps keys, bypassing the inheritance code, and diverting the encrypted files containing Silas's crimes.]


(To Silas)


We're not taking your money, Father. We're taking your freedom. Every piece of data-the fraud, the shell companies, the real murder of Vellum-it's all being pushed to the SEC and the authorities right now.

Silas

(The holographic image begins to pixelate, his voice rising in panic)


No! Stop! The controls-Sara! Push the ALERT button! Take the money!

Sara

(Suddenly calm, looking at the panel, then at Marcus)


It won't matter. He'll escape the country. The money is traceable.


[Sara steps back from the panel. She locks eyes with Marcus and Anya, a moment of shared, dark understanding passing between the true siblings.]


(To Silas, a final, chilling declaration)


You wanted an heir ruthless enough to survive. I survived. But I didn't survive to be like you. I survived to destroy you.


A heavy security door slams shut in the archive. External sirens wail loudly, closer now, echoing off the underground walls. The holographic image of Silas Bellweather shrieked a final, garbled curse before dissolving into flickering pixels.


Anya

(Leaning against the server rack, exhausted but resolute)


It's done. We didn't get the inheritance.

Marcus

(Looking at his two sisters, then at the empty space where his father stood)


We got something better. We got the truth. And we got each other. Now, let's go explain this to the police.

The fortune was never claimed.
